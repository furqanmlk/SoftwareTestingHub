## Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

### Q1
Which statement BEST describes Generative AI (GenAI)?

A. AI that follows predefined rules  
B. AI that generates new content based on learned patterns  
C. AI that executes automated test scripts  
D. AI that guarantees correct outputs  

**Answer:** B

---

### Q2

A. Generative AI  
B. Deep Learning  
C. Symbolic AI  
D. Classical Machine Learning  

**Answer:** C
